What is Filma and why is it different?

Filma is eBay’s and TikTok’s lovechild!

Filma is the first Australian interactive video commerce marketplace. Experience the new dimension of shopping with Filma.

Watch livestreams, get answers to your questions in real time, participate to exclusive events and buy products like never before.

Filma was built with the purpose of making the live-shopping technology available to the general public without any upfront cost or training required by sellers.

Why should you shop on Filma?

Welcome to the future of online shopping!

As a spectator on Filma, you can get answers to your questions in real time, watch product demos, follow your favourite producers and studios, curate your whish list and participate to exclusive events.

It’s like an in-store shopping experience from the comfort of your house.

Why should brands use Filma?

Filma offers studios a new platform to interact with their audience and build rapport. All you need is a smartphone and a laptop to get started.

Schedule your upcoming livestreams, manage your inventory, find producers to collaborate with, and download shipping labels from your personalised Filma dashboard.

How does Filma work for content creators?

Filma offers producers a new way to collaborate with studios.

Use Filma’s own casting call feature to find your next collaboration. Go live, engage with your audience, present products you love and track your performance on your personalised Filma dashboard.

Frequently asked questions

General

Who are Spectators, Producers, and Studios?

Spectators are buyers. They can buy products, ask questions, and interact with their favourite producers and studios in real time. Spectators can also follow studio and producers accounts.

Producers are content creators. They are subject matter experts that love talking about their favourite products and make money out of it.

Studios are stores/ brands. They can upload their inventory, reach out to producers, and sell live

Is Filma only available on iOS?

No, our app is available for iOS and Android devices. The webapp can be access from any device with internet access.

Are there age restrictions on Filma?

You must be 12+ to download the app. We are working on age-appropriate access for certain shows for now, we recommend keeping your content and products suitable for all ages.

For Studios and Producers

How can I collaborate with a studio or producer?

For studios: go to the casting call section within your dashboard and follow the prompts to create a new casting call and wait for producers to respond to your post.

For producers: go to the casting call section within your dashboard and browse through all the open opportunities. Follow the prompts to apply.

What analytics can you provide to sellers?

Engagement, number of orders, number of returns, compounded sales data, most popular product and more!

What analytics can you provide to producers?

Engagement, number of followers, number of orders, compounded sales data and more!

What do I need to go live?

All you need is an iOS or Android device and a laptop. We also highly recommend using a ring light to make your shows look great.

How do I go live?

We’ll soon upload a step by step guide or to our how-to videos which can be found on our Youtube channel.

How long should I go live for?

Up to 20 minutes per live stream.

How do I set up my store before going live?

Our team will help you in setting up your store. Please fill out the form (insert link) or contact us at admin@filma.live

Is there a limit to how many products I can list?

You can list up to 10 items per live stream. There’s no limitation on how many items you can have in your inventory.

Do you provide any coaching for sellers?

We provide onboarding materials for sellers to help with setting up their store and preparing for and marketing their first live show. We also provide some audience engagement metrics that help sellers strive for success and growth in future shows.

When and how often should I go live?

We recommend going live at least thrice per week to stay connected with your audience. Keeping a consistent schedule and going live regularly will help build your audience.

What is the Backstage?

The backstage is a space that is accessed automatically whilst livestreaming via laptop. The backstage function is built for studios and producers to keep an eye on inventory quantities, questions being asked in chat and livestream.

How do I promote my show?

To maximise your audience size, we recommend bringing your existing social followers and other communities across to watch your show. Having a pre-existing audience helps reinforce your store's credibility and gets everyone excited for your shows. We also promote shows via our social media channels and within the app.

Can more than one person log into a studio account?

Yes, up to 2 people can log into a studio account concurrently.

Will audience size affect my stream's quality?

No, the number of viewers watching a show does not affect the video quality, however, wi-fi or 4G strength can affect the experience.

Do customers receive an order confirmation?

Yes, your customers will receive an email from Filma with their order confirmation after your show has ended. They will also be able to track the order journey from the Filma app.

Do you support API integrations?

Yes, we support Australia Post and Sendle API integrations.

Fees and Payments

When and how do I get paid?

Payout will be available as soon as the customer has received their order. We offer payout via bank transfer.

How does Filma make money?

We align our interests with our sellers and hosts: when you make money, we make money. There are no upfront costs to use Filma, it's free to join as a seller or as a shopper! For any sales made, we charge a small service fee and payment processing fee, with a tiered service fee as your business grows. For more details, please email admin@filma.live

For Spectators

When can I make a purchase?

You can make a purchase during a live show or off a replay.

How long will my order take to arrive?

Please refer to Australia Post or Sendle website for shipping times.

What is your returns policy?

Return policies are unique to each studio on Filma. Studios’ return policies are available on their profile.

Can I pay with AfterPay?

Not at this stage but we are working on it.

How do I get reminders for live shows?

Yes, you will receive reminders from studios and producers that you follow and also from events where you expressed your interest.

Can I share a show with friends?

Yes. You can share a direct link (via email, IG, FB, and SMS) to a show before it goes live by tapping the share icon on the seller's preview video.

How do I update my personal details?

Go to your profile on the Filma app and then tap the person icon in the bottom right corner. There you can edit your personal details, such as shipping address, payment details, and so on.

How do I contact a seller?

During a live show, you can leave a comment for the seller (which the rest of the audience will be able to see).

Who do I contact with an order query?

Please email admin@filma.live with any order questions or concerns including cancellations or returns.

After the show

What happens to my livestream once it’s finished?

Your livestream will become a replay. The replay will be featured in your profile page and Filma’s home and discover sections. Customers can shop from replays. We recommend promoting replays on your social media channels – we’ll do the same too.

How soon after the show should I ship my orders?

As soon as you download the shipping label, as long dispatch times may negatively affect your rating.

Can my customers track their shipments?

Once a Filma shipping label is scanned by mail carrier, its tracking number will appear in the customer's profile. This will trigger an automated email (with their package's tracking number) that is sent to the customer letting them know that their order has shipped.

How do I contact a customer?

Sellers will not be able to contact Filma’s customers directly, you can reach us at admin@filma.live for any queries.

Shipping

How does shipping work?

Filma supports API integrations with both Sendle and Australia Post. This means that you can sync your existing Australia Post or Sendle account to Filma. You can download and print shipping labels from within your profile, directly from your device.

To ship your orders, include a packing slip and attach a shipping label to each package, and then drop them off at a post office or have a courier to collect them from your location.

Customers will receive an email from us to let them know that their order has shipped with their tracking number included. They can also find this tracking number in their Filma profile.

Who pays for the shipping?

Shipping is paid by customers when they purchase an item from your store. We recommend absorbing the cost of shipping as much as possible in order to incentivise sales.

Do you ship to other countries?

Not at this stage; but we are planning to offer international shipping in the near future.

Purchasing and order management

How can I view all my orders?

You can find the order summary in your dashboard.

How do I fulfill an order?
  1. Head to your dashboard
  2. Tap on the Print Labels button and select all orders ready to be shipped. Tap on the Download Shipping Labels button and this will generate both your packing slips and shipping labels. Include the packing slip in your package, seal it, and then attach the shipping label to the exterior of the package.
  3. Once Australia Post or Sendle has scanned the package, the customer will receive a notification and will be able to track the parcel’s journey on the Filma app.
  4. When the customer receives their package, the order status on your seller profile will update from Shipped to Complete.
What happens when a customer cancels an order?

In the rare case that a customer needs to cancel an order, we ask them to do so before the order has been posted.

What happens when a seller needs to cancel an order?

Should a seller need to cancel an order, please let us know as soon as possible by emailing admin@filma.live so that we can notify the customer and handle any refund(s).

Does Filma handle customer refunds?

Yes, we handle any potential customer refunds by coordinating with the seller and customer.
